Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Faceit Checker Bot handles data.

1. Data Collection

We collect only what is necessary: user IDs, server IDs, and Faceit stats. We do not collect message content or private data.

2. Data Usage

Data is used solely to respond to commands and fetch Faceit statistics.

3. Data Storage

No permanent storage is used. Data may be cached temporarily in memory for performance.

4. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. Stats come directly from the Faceit API.

5. Data Retention

Since nothing is permanently stored, no deletion is needed. Removing the bot stops all data usage.
